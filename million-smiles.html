🌳 About this park

Known county-wide as the 'best playground ever.' This is not hyperbole — Million Smiles Park is genuinely exceptional. The equipment is modern, varied, and thoughtfully designed for different age groups. Worth the drive from Bellingham every single time.

✨ What makes it special
Widely considered the best playground in all of Whatcom County. The name says it all.
💡 Tips from families
  • Worth the 20 minute drive from Bellingham — really
  • Bring a picnic lunch and plan to stay all afternoon
  • Older kids love the basketball and tennis courts too
